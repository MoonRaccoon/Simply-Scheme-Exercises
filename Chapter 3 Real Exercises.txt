3.9. The expression (+ 8 2) has the value 10. It is a compound expression
made up of three atoms. For this problem, write five other Scheme expressions
whose values are also the number ten:

•    	An atom
•    	Another compound expression made up of three atoms
•    	A compound expression made up of four atoms
•	A compound expression made up of an atom and two compound subexpressions
•    	Any other kind of expression

A1: 10

A2: (+ 5 5)
 
A3: (+ 3 3 4)

A4: (+ (+ 2 3) (- 7 2))

A5: (- 110 (+ 25 (* 25 3)))
